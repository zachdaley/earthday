Dear Campus Community,

New this year, EWU will be hosting a recycled art contest as part of our celebration of Earth Day 2014 on Tuesday, April 22nd. The contest is designed to engage students, faculty, and staff throughout the campus in targeting items which are commonly recycled or sent to the incinerator, and instead rescuing them for creative art.

Submitted pieces will be displayed throughout the PUB Mall on Earth Day from 9am - 2pm, with the top 3 student submissions receiving prizes for their hard work and creativity.

Recycled Art Submissions

  1. Participants must have their ideas submitted by April 15th, 2014.
  2. Project must consist of 90% recycled material.
  3. Use of glue and tape must be minimal.
  4. Art will be judged on materials used, creativity, and overall aesthetic quality.
  5. Proposals must include the size of the project AND if it is free standing or if the project will require a table.
  6. Submit idea proposals to admissions@ewu.edu

Faculty -- please announce this event to your students!

Happy Recycling!
